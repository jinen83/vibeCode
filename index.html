If you look at the software landscape of any major company, it generally breaks down into three buckets:

  1. Core Business Apps: These run the bank. Think Loan Origination Systems (LOS) or SAP. You don't build these; you buy COTS.
  2. Customer Facing Apps: The shiny mobile apps and websites your customers see.
  3. Internal Software & Tools: The messy, custom, absolutely critical glue that holds operations together.

The first two get all the budget. The third one? That's where the problem lives.
